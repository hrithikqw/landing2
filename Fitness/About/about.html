About FitTrip

We're on a mission to keep travelers fit and healthy by connecting them with the best gyms around the world.

Our Mission

Making fitness accessible while you travel

At FitTrip, we believe that maintaining your fitness routine shouldn't stop just because you're away from home. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, staying active is crucial for both physical and mental wellness.

Our mission is to eliminate the barriers between travelers and quality fitness facilities, creating a global network of accessible gyms that can be booked with just a few taps.

We partner with gyms of all sizes, from boutique fitness studios to international chains, ensuring that no matter where you are, you'll find a place to work out that meets your needs and preferences.

FitTrip isn't just an app—it's a community of like-minded individuals who refuse to compromise on their fitness goals, even when on the road.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Global Access

We believe fitness should be available worldwide, without borders or barriers. Our network spans across countries and continents to ensure you never have to skip a workout.

Community First

We prioritize building a community of travelers who support each other's fitness journeys, sharing tips and celebrating achievements across different locations.

Simplicity

Traveling is complicated enough. That's why we've designed FitTrip to be intuitive and hassle-free, letting you focus on your workout, not figuring out an app.

Fair Partnership

We create win-win relationships with our gym partners, helping them fill unused capacity while giving travelers affordable access to quality facilities.
